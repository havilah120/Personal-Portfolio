CampusCart

Student Shopping App • UI/UX Case Study

Project Overview

CampusCart is a student-focused shopping platform designed to make buying and selling within a university campus easier and more accessible. The goal of this project was to create a centralized app where students can discover campus businesses, shop conveniently, and connect with vendors in one place.

The Problem

Students often struggle to find reliable campus vendors, compare prices, or discover available services within their school environment. Existing solutions were scattered across social media platforms, lacked structure, and made it difficult for students to shop efficiently or trust vendors.

The Solution

CampusCart was designed as a clean, intuitive mobile-first platform that brings campus businesses into one organized space. The design focuses on simplicity, clear navigation, and an enjoyable shopping experience while supporting student entrepreneurs and encouraging community engagement.
